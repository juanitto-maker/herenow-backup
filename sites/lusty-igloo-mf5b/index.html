❌ The Problem
⛽
Massive fuel burn
Industrial farm machinery is one of the largest non-transport fossil fuel consumers globally.
💨
Hidden emissions
Agriculture accounts for ~10-12% of global GHG — machinery fuel rarely counted in food carbon footprint.
🏜️
Ecological deserts
Monoculture at scale eliminates biodiversity, depletes soil, kills pollinators.
💸
Rising fuel costs
Farm profitability is hostage to oil prices — a structural vulnerability every decade.
✅ The Spiral Solution
🌀
Archimedean spiral rail
One continuous groove from outer edge to hub. No switching needed. Machine follows it like a record needle — inward while working, outward to reset.
🏗️
Hub as harvest sink
Machine naturally arrives at the central hub silo at end of each pass. No perimeter harvest road — just one access road per unit to hub.
☀️
Solar outer channel
Outermost spiral turn is a solar-covered irrigation channel, generating electricity powering the whole unit.
🌿
Built-in ecology
Interstitial zones between tiled spiral units become ponds, microforests, beehives — biodiversity by geometric necessity.
