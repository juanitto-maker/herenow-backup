04
Prototype Recipe

≈30 Bonbons
First Batch

Panela (powdered)50g
Cacao powder30g
Raw honey40g
Beeswax (melted)8g
Olive oil15g
Clove powder2g
Cinnamon3g
Salt (fine)0.5g
Black pepper0.3g
1
Powder panela finely in mortar
2
Melt beeswax + olive oil on lowest heat
3
Off heat — add honey, panela, cacao, spices
4
Mix into thick paste, roll marble-sized balls
5
Refrigerate 1h · Store in glass jar · Lasts 3–4 weeks
05
Texture Options

Choose Your
Chew

Chewiness can be tuned with natural additives:

🌾
Oat Flour
Dense, bread-like chew. Grind rolled oats.
🌿
Fig Paste
Sticky taffy-like pull. 3-4 dried figs mashed.
🫙
Agar Agar
Jelly/Turkish delight style. V2 format.
🌱
Psyllium Husk
Doughy, elastic. Just 2–3g needed.
🌰
Ground Almonds
Crumbly-then-chewy. Local & abundant.
🟫
Carob Powder
Sticky, sweet, Mediterranean. Algarroba.
