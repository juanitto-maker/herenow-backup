The idea, in 30 seconds

Replace WordPress and inbox chaos with one integrated system. A chatbot handles clients around the clock. A phone-camera capture flow turns a property walkthrough into a polished listing in minutes. You stay in control — the system handles the repetition.

How to try it

Open the link, click everywhere, break things. You'll figure it out — but here's a quick tour if you want one.

Quick tour (optional)
  1. Open the prototype site in a new tab.
  2. Browse the properties — click any listing to see its full page.
  3. Try the chatbot — look for the bubble in the bottom-right corner. Ask it anything.
  4. Switch language at the top — the whole site is translated (ES / EN / FR / DE).
  5. The admin panel (create properties, manage leads) needs an invite — see section 10 below.
