A note from the founder

If you've found this page, something resonated.

digitalAIventures is a one-person studio with a portfolio mind. I build real things — software shipped to clients, hardware concepts pushed through defensive publication, trade work bringing CE-grade equipment from China to Europe, and a steady stream of ideas that get prototyped, documented, and put online for anyone to find.

The base is a quiet patch of Andalucía. Low burn, focused work, no office overhead, no team to manage — just output. The whole stack runs from a phone: GitHub web, Claude as collaborator, Vercel and Supabase doing the heavy lifting. It's a constraint that became a method.

Some projects are revenue-bearing today. Some are deep R&D plays where the moat is being first to publish. Some are open-source infrastructure I use to run everything else. They share a thread: solving real problems with the smallest possible footprint.

What's missing is partners. Not employees, not co-founders in the rigid sense — partners. People who pick a project that lights them up and bring whatever they bring: capital, a phone number, a contact, a skill, a piece of land, an afternoon. The studio is structured to absorb that. Equity, revenue share, advisory shares, finder's fees, JV splits — whichever shape fits.

If anything in the portfolio below speaks to you, the door is open.

Juanitto Calvares Founder · digitalAIventures LLC

Ways to partner

Capital is one path. There are six others.

Pick a project, pick a lane. Every partnership is shaped to fit — equity, revenue share, advisory shares, finder's fee, JV split, or just a handshake.

💰
Capitalangels · cheques · grants

From €2k seed cheques for a single project to larger commitments for a portfolio slice. Project-specific funding works as well as studio-level. Equity, SAFE, or revenue-share — your shape.

🤝
Connectorsintros · referrals · finders

Open doors to investors, clients, or strategic partners. Finder's fees on closed deals, retainers for ongoing introductions. Especially valuable: olive industry, datacenter, real estate, manufacturing.

🛠️
Buildersdevs · designers · writers

Pick a project, ship something. Frontend, backend, design, content, translation, QA — paid hourly, project-rate, or revenue-share. Bring your craft to a real product, not a roadmap deck.

📋
Operatorslegal · ops · cert · IP

EU entity setup, accounting, CE certification, patent prosecution, contracts. The boring layer that unlocks everything else. Retainer or equity, depending on scope.

🧠
Domain expertsindustry insiders

Deep knowledge in olive oil, datacenters, real estate, manufacturing, agriculture, energy, or building science. Advisory shares, board-style guidance, or paid consulting.

🌍
Ground partnersland · sites · prototypes

Land for AgroRail pilots, lake access for DeepData, olive groves for VacOil, host sites for PearlShell shelters. Real-world testbeds in exchange for equity, royalty, or simply a working prototype on your land.

🚀
Joint venturerstake a project · run it · split it

Take any single project — VacOil, DeepData, ButterViz, DramaGenieAI, anything — and run it as your own venture under a revenue or equity split. The studio holds the IP scaffolding; you bring the operator energy. White-label, co-brand, or full spinout — open to discussion.
