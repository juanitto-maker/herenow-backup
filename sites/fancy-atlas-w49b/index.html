The Opportunity
The Pitch
"Fast shelters have no insulation. Insulated shelters take weeks. PearlShell solves both."
The Problem
Emergency housing, glamping, farm worker accommodation and off-grid living all share the same frustration: speed and insulation have never coexisted in one affordable system.
Our Solution
PearlShell combines off-the-shelf galvanised steel tunnel frames with pre-filled volcanic perlite insulation bags — a fully weatherproof, insulated shelter in one working day, two people, no specialist skills.
The Value
€280–400 in materials. €1,200–1,600 installed price. A system any trained team can replicate, indefinitely.
The Ask
We are inviting a small group of early partners — glamping operators, rural developers, NGOs — to co-build the first PearlShell units in Andalucía and prove the model together.
The System
Four components
Each chosen for speed, performance and local sourcing
🏗️
Galvanised steel arch frame
Off-the-shelf 3×3×2m tunnel kit. Assembles in under one hour with a single spanner. Ground-staked, wind-braced, no concrete required.
🌿
Perlite-filled jute bags
90×60cm natural jute sacks filled with expanded volcanic perlite — the lightest mineral insulator available. Batch-prepared off-site, arrive ready to stack.
🎋
Bamboo spacer rail system
Horizontal bamboo poles lashed to the arch legs hold the outer tarp clear of the insulation — creating a ventilated drainage cavity that protects the bags long-term.
🏕️
Heavy-duty PVC tarp roof
650 g/m² truck-grade PVC over the arch profile. UV-resistant, fully waterproof. 10–15 year outdoor lifespan. Replaceable independently of the structure.
