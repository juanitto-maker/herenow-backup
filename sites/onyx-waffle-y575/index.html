01 — The Model Family

Four Models, Every Scale

From a phone in your pocket to a workstation under your desk — one family covers it all.

📱

E2B — Ultra Edge

2.3B effective params. Runs on phones, Raspberry Pi, any 2GB+ device. Text + Image + Audio. 128K context. The smallest model with native speech recognition.

💻

E4B — Edge Pro

4.5B effective params. Laptops and tablets with 8GB RAM. Text + Image + Audio. 128K context. Complex tasks without internet.

⚡

26B A4B — The MoE Star

26B total, only 3.8B active per token. Runs at 4B speed, thinks at 30B quality. 256K context. The efficiency breakthrough — best quality-per-watt in open AI.

🖥️

31B Dense — Maximum

31B params, full dense architecture. Top quality for demanding workloads. 256K context. #3 open model globally on LMArena.
