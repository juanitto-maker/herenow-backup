The Technology

Vacuum Tray Drying — The Core Innovation

Conventional drying destroys polyphenols with heat and oxygen. Our Vacuum Tray Drying (VTD) system removes moisture at 30–40°C under near-zero atmospheric pressure, preserving the bioactive compounds that command premium prices.

Parameter Conventional Drying Vacuum Tray Drying (VTD) ✓
Temperature60–80°C30–40°C
Oxygen exposureHigh (open air)Near-zero (vacuum)
Drying time8–12 hours4–6 hours
Polyphenol retention40–60%85–95%
Microbial safetyRisk if under-driedVacuum inhibits growth

Two Processing Paths

🫒
Path A
Centrifugal De-Pitter + VTD + Press
Pit removed before drying → flesh-only paste → maximum polyphenol potential (900–1,200 mg/kg). Requires 2 de-pitter units at 500 kg/day and press aids. Higher CAPEX (€155k), slower press cycle.
Maximum Polyphenols
🔨
Path B RECOMMENDED
Hammer Mill + VTD + Press
Whole olive crushed (flesh + pit) → pit fragments = natural press aid → no bottleneck, faster cycle, lower cost (€140k). Polyphenols 5–10% lower but still 820–1,100 mg/kg — well above UHP threshold. Optional pit screen for premium batches.
★ Best ROI · No Bottleneck
